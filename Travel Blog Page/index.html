📍 Trip to Manali

Visited: March 2024

Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh, known for its snowy mountains, cozy cafes, and adventure sports.

Things I Did:

  • River Rafting in Beas
  • Visited Solang Valley
  • Enjoyed local Himachali food

What I Learned:

Traveling solo taught me how to plan, stay safe, and enjoy nature mindfully. I also met some amazing fellow travelers!

📍 Trip to Jaipur

Visited: December 2023

Jaipur, the Pink City, was full of history, colors, and food! I visited forts, palaces, and shopped for traditional clothes and jewelry.

Top Highlights:

  • Amber Fort light and sound show
  • Street food at Masala Chowk
  • Shopping in Bapu Bazaar
